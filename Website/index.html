What is our application for?

We made our application to help people (mostly students) learn more about the body systems.
 Our program works this way:

You will be given an essential information about one of the body system


You will have to solve a quiz to test what you have learned


You are only able to learn about a different body system after you have solved the quiz about the previous one.

Hope you enjoy our program!
